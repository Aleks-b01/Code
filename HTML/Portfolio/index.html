Speedcubing

Speedcubing is a hobby centered around solving Rubik's Cubesas fast as possible. Speedcubing has been my main hobby and passion for over 7 years, and has been a core part of my life.

Most of this time I have specialized in solving the 3x3 puzzle, which is the most common type of the Rubik's Cube, however in recent time I have found a deep passion in blindfolded solving, now being able to solve a 3x3 cube blindfolded in under 30 seconds, and sometimes even in under 20 seconds.

Currently I am learning a technique called Full Floating, which consists of learning and maintaining over 2.700 intuitive algorithms, as well as a technique called LTCT (Last target corner twist), which consists of learning and maintaining 252 non-intuitive algorithms. If you'd like to learn specifics like my Personal Best times, you can visit my speedcubing sheet by going to https://bit.ly/alekscubing.
