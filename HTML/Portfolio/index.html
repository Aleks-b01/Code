About me

Technical Background

For over two years I've been deeply passionate about programming, working on multiple personal projects in various fields, and learning a wide range of valuable skills along the way.

Most of this time I've invested in web development, which is the field I'm most comfortable with and want to focus on in the future.

I also want to expand my skill set as much as possible, by learning frameworks like React, perfecting my knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and by pushing myself to write cleaner, and more efficient code.

Speedcubing

Speedcubing is a hobby centered around solving Rubik's Cubes as quickly as possible. It has been my main hobby and passion for over seven years, and has been a core part of my life.

For most of that time, I've specialized in solving the 3x3 cube, the most common type of the Rubik's Cube. However, I've recently developed a deep passion in blindfolded solving, now solving a 3x3 cube blindfolded in under 30 seconds, and occasionally in under 20 seconds.

I'm currently learning an advanced technique called Full Floating, which involves memorizing and maintaining over 2,700 intuitive algorithms. I'm also learning an algorithm set called LTCT (Last Target Corner Twist), which consists of 252 non-intuitive algorithms.

If you're curious about specifics like my personal best times, feel free to check out my speedcubing sheet.
